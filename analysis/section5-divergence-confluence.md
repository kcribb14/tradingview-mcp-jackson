## 5.5 OBV (On-Balance Volume) Analysis

**Current OBV:** 1.75M

### OBV Interpretation Framework

OBV accumulates volume on up days and subtracts volume on down days. The trend of OBV reveals whether smart money is accumulating or distributing beneath the surface of price action.

| OBV Scenario | Price Action | Signal |
|---|---|---|
| OBV making higher lows while price consolidates | Sideways / declining | **Accumulation (Bullish)** |
| OBV declining with price | Price falling | **Distribution (Bearish)** |
| OBV making higher highs with price | Price rising | **Trend confirmation** |
| OBV making lower highs while price rises | Price rising | **Hidden bearish divergence** |

### Current OBV Assessment

With OBV at 1.75M and the capitulation event having generated 12,392 volume units, the OBV reflects the net directional flow across the 60-day window.

**Critical observation:** The massive capitulation volume on Jun 6 would have created a sharp OBV decline (if it was a red close) or a spike (if it closed green despite the intraday low). The subsequent OBV trend direction from that point forward determines whether accumulation is occurring.

**Verdict: NEUTRAL — Directional Confirmation Required**
Without the OBV trend from the Jun 6 low clearly mapped, a definitive call cannot be made. The indicator requires monitoring for:
- Higher OBV lows as price makes higher lows = **hidden accumulation**
- Falling OBV as price stabilizes = **distribution into weakness**
