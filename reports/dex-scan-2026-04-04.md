### Key Insight
Every token shows **DEX more bullish than CEX**. This is structural:
- CEX F&G uses 200-day EMA deviation (lagging, reflects trend)
- DEX F&G uses real-time order flow + liquidity (leading, reflects sentiment)
- In a down-trending market, CEX shows FEAR (price below long-term avg)
  while DEX shows NEUTRAL (buy/sell flow is balanced, no panic selling)
- When this divergence flips (DEX shows fear while CEX still bullish) = distribution signal
